Epic Ease

Epic Ease, a project I developed, introduced a template tailored to our client's needs, making it easier for the business team to map out user flows quickly. This move cut down feedback time by ~30%, making the whole process more efficient. The outcome was a more collaborative work environment where everyone could contribute to improving project quality and meeting deadlines.
