Fit Fusion

Context aware Android Application

Fit Fusion is an android mobile application my team and I built to provide users with a personalised fitness experience. The app syncs with an android wearbale device to monitor health metrics while the user is working out and adjusts the remaining workout routine in real-time to suit his/her requirements. For instance, if your heart rate suddenly goes up, the app tells you to take a break. And if you're finding the workout easy, it makes it harder to keep up with your level.
