Makershala

I developed and rolled out an e-learning platform centered around STEM subjects, employing HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and PHP. Tailored for youngsters between the ages of 8 and 16, this platform goes beyond traditional education. It serves as a dynamic space where kids can not only delve into their interests but also cultivate crucial skills and deepen their grasp of STEM concepts. Through seamless integration of technology and education, the project strives to make learning an exciting journey, laying the groundwork for future success in STEM fields.

Epic Ease

Epic Ease, a project I developed, introduced a template tailored to our client's needs, making it easier for the business team to map out user flows quickly. This move cut down feedback time by ~30%, making the whole process more efficient. The template not only helped the business team but also made it simpler for the development team to understand user flows better. It was a practical solution that boosted overall project productivity and improved collaboration between different teams, emphasizing my dedication to finding effective solutions in project management.

Bugs Eye

Bugs Eye was a 3-tier web application I built to simplify bug tracking and resolution, resulting in an impressive 80% reduction in rework across teams. This project focused on making the bug management process more straightforward, helping teams identify and fix issues faster. The outcome was a more collaborative work environment where everyone could contribute to improving project quality and meeting deadlines.
